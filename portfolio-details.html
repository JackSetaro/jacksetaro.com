Gaben Club is fan Site

A Gabe Newell Fan Site

Gaben club was the first website I made. It was based on another site of the same name. That site used to play Gabe Newell remixed music any time you refreshed the page. This website was a test for me to practice making websites as well as working with javascript. The website has gone through many iterations, however I still plan on updating it to fix the problems with new knowledge I have learned.
